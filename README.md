# hackathon-project-
Project Introduction

AKTU OneStop is a comprehensive, data-driven academic portal designed to streamline exam preparation for students at Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University. By moving away from fragmented study materials, our platform utilizes Python-based frequency analysis to identify and rank high-priority topics from previous years' question papers. Our goal is to eliminate "last-minute panic" by providing a structured,"Emergency Study Mode" that focuses on high-weightage concepts, ensuring students spend their limited time on what actually matters for their CGPA.
